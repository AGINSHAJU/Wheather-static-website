Measures to Take

Stay Hydrated

In hot weather, it's crucial to drink plenty of water to avoid dehydration. Carry a water bottle and take regular sips throughout the day.

Dress Appropriately

Check the weather forecast and dress accordingly. Layer up in cold conditions and opt for light, breathable fabrics when it's hot.

Seek Shelter

During severe weather warnings, find a safe place indoors. Stay away from windows and avoid using electrical appliances.
